Best Model: Count vectoriser + Multinomial Naïve Bayes

The pipeline with the highest performance consisted of CountVectorizer and Multinomial Naive Bayes. Such an outcome implies that the sentiment in the data set was primarily determined by word usage as opposed to more complex interdependencies. Considering the limited number of entries in the data set, simpler probabilistic models have been found sufficiently capable of generalization without increasing complexity.
